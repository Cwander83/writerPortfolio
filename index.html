about section

      Jeremy Williams is an American author best known for the “Running Backward” book series. Currently residing in his hometown, Orlando, Florida, Williams is always inspired by his surroundings which guide him in writing dozens of short, fictional tales, taking everyday occurrences and intertwining them with science fiction- or fantasy-based origins. “Running Backward”, a book series about black history, Latin history, and time travel is Williams’ first collection of full-length novels. Williams also focuses on, but is not limited to, urban characters as main characters for his stories such as “It Takes Two” and “The Architect”, with the reasoning that “representation matters.”
